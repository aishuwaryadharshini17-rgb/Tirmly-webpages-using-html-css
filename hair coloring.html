  • Balayage – A natural-looking hand-painted coloring technique.
  • Ombre – A gradual color transition from dark roots to lighter ends.
  • Global Hair Color – A uniform hair color applied to all hair strands.
  • Fashion Colors – Bold shades like red, burgundy, purple, or blue.

Important Rules of Hair Coloring

To achieve the best results and maintain healthy hair, it is important to follow certain hair coloring rules and precautions.

  • Always consult a professional stylist before choosing a hair color.
  • Select a hair color that suits your skin tone and natural hair color.
  • Perform a patch test before applying any hair color to avoid allergic reactions.
  • Use high-quality hair coloring products to prevent hair damage.
  • Avoid frequent coloring, as excessive coloring can weaken hair.
  • Follow proper hair care routines after coloring to maintain color longevity.
  • Use sulfate-free shampoos designed for colored hair.
  • Regularly condition your hair to maintain softness and shine.
  • Protect colored hair from excessive sunlight and heat styling tools.
  • Visit the salon for touch-ups to maintain consistent color.
