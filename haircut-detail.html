Popular Haircut Types

Layer Cut

Layer cut adds movement and volume to your hair. It is one of the most popular hairstyles for women because it suits almost all face shapes and hair types.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is a timeless and elegant hairstyle. It gives a modern and professional look and is perfect for women who prefer short hair.

Step Cut

Step cut provides a layered look where the hair is cut in steps. It gives a stylish and trendy appearance.

Feather Cut

Feather haircut creates a soft and light texture by feathering the hair. This style gives a voluminous and stylish look.

Pixie Cut

Pixie cut is a bold and fashionable hairstyle. It is very short but extremely stylish and perfect for confident women.

U Cut

U cut is perfect for long hair lovers. The hair is shaped like the letter U, which makes the hair look thick and smooth.
